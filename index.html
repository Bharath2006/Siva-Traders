Our Expertise

Specialized Trims Processing

Enhancing your apparel with precision winding, tape processing, and professional fabric fusing. Designed to match strict garment manufacturing requirements.

Precision Tape Winding

High-accuracy tape winding designed to maintain consistent tension and smooth output. Ideal for garment tapes, elastics, and lace materials that require flawless feeding in production lines.

Industrial Twill Winding

Robust twill winding solution built for bulk garment manufacturing needs. Perfect for drawstrings, neck tapes, and heavy-duty applications with uniform winding and high efficiency.

Specialized Ball Winding

Neat and compact ball winding crafted for thread presentation and easy usability. Best suited for embroidery threads, retail packing, and premium garment detailing.

Advanced Garment Fusing

High-precision garment fusing ensuring strong adhesion with zero bubbling. Ideal for collars, cuffs, and plackets delivering a clean, professional finish for quality garments.

Custom Trims Repacking

Bespoke reconing, rewinding, and customized batching of garment accessories, designed to integrate seamlessly into your production line.

Rigorous QC Inspection

Strict quality control protocols to eliminate defects and ensure uniform trim integrity, providing a flawless foundation for garment finishing.
